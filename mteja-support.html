Frequently Asked Questions

  • Is my data safe?
    Yes. All your financial data is stored locally on your device. We never upload, collect, or access your personal information.
  • Do I need an account?
    No. Mteja Smart works entirely offline with no login required.
  • How do I change my currency?
    Go to Settings from the Home screen and select your preferred currency.

    Go to Settings and choose to reset your security PIN.

    The AI Advisor analyzes your budget data on-device and provides personalized insights, suggestions, and alerts based on your spending patterns.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 17, 2026

Introduction

Mteja Smart ("the App") is developed by Mteja Smart Financial Services ("we", "our", "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information.

Data Collection

Mteja Smart does NOT collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers. All data you enter into the app — including your profile information, budget data, bills, savings goals, and family member details — is stored exclusively on your device using local storage.

What We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect: names, email addresses, financial information, usage analytics, location data, device identifiers, or any other personally identifiable information. We have no servers, databases, or cloud infrastructure that stores user data.

Profile Information

During onboarding, you may provide personal details such as your name, date of birth, career information, goals, family members, and a profile photo. This information is stored only on your device to personalize your experience. It is never transmitted to us or any third party.

Photos

If you upload a profile photo, it is stored locally on your device. It is not uploaded to any server.

Notifications

The app may send local notifications for bill reminders and spending alerts. These notifications are generated on your device and do not involve any server communication.

Third-Party Services

Mteja Smart does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Data Security

Your data is protected by your device's built-in security features. The app offers optional PIN and biometric (Face ID/Touch ID) authentication for additional security.

Children's Privacy

The app is suitable for users aged 13 and above. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Data Deletion

You can delete all app data at any time through the Settings screen. Uninstalling the app will also remove all stored data permanently.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected in the app and on this page with an updated date.

Contact Us
